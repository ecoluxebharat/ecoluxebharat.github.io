About Us

Ecoluxe Bharat is a multidisciplinary design and build studio, crafting spaces where luxury meets sustainability. From concept to execution, we deliver thoughtful, functional, and future-forward environments.

We bring together interior and exterior design, landscaping, metal fabrication, acoustics, solar solutions, and custom furniture under one seamless experience. Whether it’s a private residence or a commercial workspace, every project reflects our passion for detail, innovation, and ecological responsibility.

Our Mission

To design and deliver exceptional spaces that elevate lives — by integrating luxury aesthetics, smart engineering, and sustainable practices in every project we undertake.

Our Vision

To be a leading force in India’s sustainable design evolution — creating environments that not only inspire, but also stand as models for responsible and elegant living.

Why choose us?

Our ability to bring outstanding results to our customers.
